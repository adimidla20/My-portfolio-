Projects

Current and upcoming projects that solve real problems I face as an engineering student.

COMING SOON

🔍 Exam Scanner

The Problem: Engineering exam papers contain complex diagrams, mathematical equations, and multi-format questions that are difficult to digitize and organize for exam preparation.

My Solution: An AI-powered scanner that can extract text, recognize diagrams, and intelligently organize exam papers into searchable, study-friendly formats.

Planned Features:

• OCR for handwritten and printed text extraction

• Diagram recognition and categorization

• Auto-tagging by subject and difficulty level

• Export to markdown or PDF with clean formatting

Tech Stack: Python, OpenCV, Tesseract OCR, AI Image Recognition APIs

COMING SOON

🍽️ Hostel Menu Planner

The Problem: Hostel mess menus are unpredictable, and students often miss their favorite meals or don't know what's being served until they arrive.

My Solution: A smart menu tracker that scrapes hostel mess schedules, sends notifications for favorite dishes, and helps students plan their meals efficiently.

Planned Features:

• Daily menu notifications via Telegram/WhatsApp

• Personal preference tracking ("notify me when paneer is served")

• Nutritional information lookup

• Weekly meal planner and shopping list generator

Tech Stack: Python, Web Scraping (BeautifulSoup), Telegram Bot API, SQLite
